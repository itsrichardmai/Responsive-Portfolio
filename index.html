    I love learning and mastering new things! The excitement from finally conceptualizing and getting a boost in momentum is the greatest thing to me. Perhaps the most important thing is not what you do, where you do it, or even when you do it but rather, how and why you do it. Tasks that are too hard tempt anxiety. Tasks too easy tempt boredom. The perfect balance is where we acheive Flow. We start our days completing the easy tasks of the day. The difficulty of the challenges are very minimal and the skill required is very minimal. With the momentum generated from completing the easy tasks, the mind feels a lot more confident to take on harder problems.
